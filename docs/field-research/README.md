
# Field Research
## Overview
Before writing a single line of code, SETU was validated through direct
conversations
with the people it is built for: headmasters, teachers, wardens, and
Regional
Coordinators (RCOs) of TGTWREIS Gurukulam schools in Telangana.
## Why Field Research?
The author is a former Gurukulam student. The problems SETU addresses
are not
hypothetical — they are lived experiences. This research was conducted
to:
1. Validate that the problem is current and widespread (not just from
memory)
2. Understand what officials actually need, not what we assume they
need
3. Ensure every design decision is grounded in real user behaviour
4. Build trust with the institutions who will eventually use this
system
## Methodology
- Semi-structured interviews (30–45 minutes) using an interview guide
- Written questionnaires distributed to school staff for independent
completion
- Observations during school visits
## Ethics & Privacy
- All participants were briefed on the purpose of research before
participating
- Participation was voluntary
- All published notes are anonymised (schools referred to as School
A/B/C)
- No individual questionnaire responses are published — only aggregate
summaries
- Raw data is not stored in this repository
