# UX Principles for SETU 
 
## Who The Users Are 
 
SETU is built for three types of users in Telangana's TGTWREIS school 
system: 
1. School staff (headmasters, teachers, wardens) — primary reporters 
2. District officials — primary responders 
3. State administrators — oversight and accountability 
 
## Design Constraints from Field Research 
 - **Connectivity**: Many schools have unreliable 3G. Pages must load 
under 5 seconds on 3G. - **Device**: Staff use Android smartphones. Desktop is secondary. - **Language**: Telugu is the first language. All labels available in 
Telugu. - **Literacy**: Forms designed for users who may have difficulty with 
long English text. - **Trust**: Users are cautious about 'official' systems. Anonymity 
options are prominent. 
 
## Principles 
 
1. Every action must be completable in under 3 taps on mobile 
2. Status must be visible without logging in (via shareable issue link) 
3. No information hidden behind confusing menus 
4. Error messages in plain language, not technical codes
